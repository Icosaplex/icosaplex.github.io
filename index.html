Projects

Steemit Battleships

Our first project was an implementation of the classic Battleships game on the Steemit Social network. Each day a new round was posted. Players would comment with their move. When the post paid out, the payout was shared among winning players. The main aim of this project was to explore ways of leveraging existing cryptocurrency and blockchain technology to generate player rewards for online games.
